Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Summary: Windsurfer Tracker collects your GPS location during races so that race organizers and spectators can see your position on a live map. Your location is visible to anyone viewing the race.

1. Introduction

Windsurfer Tracker ("the App") is a GPS tracking application designed for windsurfing races and water sports events. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile applications (Android, iOS, Wear OS) and web services.

By using the App, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Name or Identifier: A name, nickname, or sail number you enter to identify yourself on the tracking map
  • Role: Your role in the event (sailor, support boat, spectator)
  • Event Selection: Which event/race you are participating in

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Data Type Purpose
GPS Location To display your position on the race map
Speed & Heading To show your direction and velocity
Timestamp To record when each position was reported
Device Battery Level To help race organizers monitor tracker status
Signal Strength To assess connection quality
App Version For troubleshooting and compatibility
Heart Rate (Wear OS only) Optional health data if sensor available

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your email address, phone number, or account credentials
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • We do not track your location when the app is not actively tracking
  • We do not use advertising trackers or analytics services

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the purpose of race tracking:

  • Live Tracking: Display your position on the public race map in real-time
  • Safety Monitoring: Allow race organizers to monitor participant locations and respond to assist requests
  • Race Review: Enable playback and analysis of race tracks after the event
  • Statistics: Calculate race statistics such as speed and distance

4. Who Can See Your Data

Important: Your location data is publicly visible to anyone viewing the race on wstracker.org. Do not use this app if you do not want your location to be publicly visible during the event.
  • Public Viewers: Anyone can view the live tracking map and see participant positions
  • Race Organizers: Have additional access to track history, assist alerts, and participant management
  • Other Participants: Can see your position on the map along with their own

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Server Location: Data is stored on servers in Australia
  • Retention Period: Track logs are retained for the duration of the event and may be kept for historical/archival purposes
  • No Account Required: We do not maintain user accounts; your data is associated only with the name/identifier you provide

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

  • Event passwords are required for tracker apps to submit location data
  • Administrative functions are protected by separate passwords
  • Data transmission uses standard internet protocols

However, as location data is publicly displayed on the tracking map, you should not expect privacy for your location while using this service.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Start/Stop Tracking: You control when the app tracks your location by pressing Start/Stop
  • Name Choice: You can use any name or identifier; it does not need to be your real name
  • Uninstall: You can stop using the service at any time by uninstalling the app
  • Data Deletion: Contact the race organizers to request removal of your track data

8. Background Location

The App uses background location permission so that tracking continues while:

  • Your phone screen is locked or off
  • You are using other apps
  • You are actively sailing and cannot interact with the phone

Location tracking only occurs when you have explicitly started tracking. The app does not track your location at any other time.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for participants in organized water sports events. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a child uses the App, they should do so under the supervision of a parent or guardian who understands and accepts this privacy policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact:

  • Website: wstracker.org
  • Email: windsurfertracker@gmail.com

For event-specific data requests, please contact the race organizers directly.
