Getting Help

1. Contact Your Event Administrator

For questions about a specific event, the best first step is to contact the person who gave you the event password or event link. They can help with:

  • Event-specific settings and passwords
  • Which server URL to use
  • Your role assignment (sailor, support, spectator)
  • Local event procedures

2. Join the Community

Join our Google Group to ask questions, share feedback, and connect with other users and developers:

To subscribe: Send an email to wstracker+subscribe@googlegroups.com

To post: Send an email to wstracker@googlegroups.com

This is the best place for general questions, feature requests, and community discussion.

3. Email Support

For issues that require direct assistance, you can email us:

windsurfertracker@gmail.com

Reporting Issues

Important: When reporting a problem, please include the following information to help us diagnose the issue quickly.

Required Information

  • App Version: Find this on the Settings page of the app (scroll down to see the version number, e.g., "v1.2.3 (abc1234)")
  • Device: Your phone/watch model (e.g., "iPhone 14", "Samsung Galaxy S23", "Galaxy Watch 5")
  • Operating System: iOS version, Android version, or Wear OS version

Helpful Details

  • Screenshots: Attach screenshots showing the issue or any error messages
  • Steps to reproduce: What were you doing when the problem occurred?
  • Event details: Which event were you tracking? (if applicable)
  • Frequency: Does this happen every time, or only sometimes?
Tip: The more details you provide, the faster we can help resolve your issue!

Common Questions

The app isn't sending my location

  • Check that you've pressed "Start Tracking"
  • Ensure location permissions are granted (including background location)
  • Verify you have mobile data or WiFi connectivity
  • Check that the server URL and event password are correct

My position isn't showing on the map

  • Wait a few seconds - there may be network delay
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try refreshing the map page
  • Verify you're viewing the correct event

Battery drain concerns

  • GPS tracking does use battery - this is normal
  • Consider using a battery pack for long events
  • The app only uses GPS when you're actively tracking
