Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Windsurfer Tracker

Device Compatibility

Why don't you support Garmin watches?

Unfortunately there are two issues with Garmin watches that prevent us from supporting them:

  • LTE connectivity: Only the very top-end (and very expensive) Garmin watches have LTE capability. Without LTE, the watch cannot send live position updates to the tracking server - it would need to be paired with a phone, which defeats the purpose of standalone watch tracking.
  • Foreground-only apps: Even on Garmin watches that do have LTE, apps can only do live tracking when running in the "foreground". This means the tracker would need to take over the entire watch screen while sailing, preventing you from using your watch for other things like race timing.

In contrast, Apple Watch and Wear OS watches allow apps to track in the background while you use other watch features. For these reasons, we don't plan to port Windsurfer Tracker to the Garmin ecosystem at this stage.

Which devices are supported?

Windsurfer Tracker currently supports:

  • iPhone: iOS 15 or later
  • Apple Watch: watchOS 9 or later (with cellular or paired to iPhone)
  • Android phones: Android 8.0 or later
  • Wear OS watches: Wear OS 3.0 or later (with LTE)
Note: For standalone watch tracking (without a phone), you need a watch with LTE/cellular capability.
