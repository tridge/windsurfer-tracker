# Windsurfer Tracker - App Store Descriptions

## App Name
**Windsurfer Tracker**

## Short Description (80 characters max)
GPS race tracking for windsurfing events with live map and safety features.

## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Windsurfer Tracker is a GPS tracking app designed for windsurfing races and water sports events. Share your position in real-time with race organizers and spectators watching on the live map at wstracker.org.

**Key Features:**

- Real-time GPS tracking every 10 seconds
- Live position display on web-based race map
- Request Assist button for on-water emergencies
- Battery-efficient background tracking
- Works with minimal mobile signal
- Support for multiple concurrent events
- Track history and race replay

**How It Works:**

1. Get the event name and password from race organizers
2. Enter your name or sail number
3. Tap Start Tracking before you launch
4. Your position appears on the live map
5. Spectators and safety boats can follow your progress

**Perfect For:**

- Windsurfing races and regattas
- Sailing events
- Paddleboard races
- Any water sports event needing participant tracking
- Race safety and support coordination

**Safety First:**

The Request Assist feature lets you alert race organizers if you need help on the water. Your position is immediately highlighted on the map so support boats can find you quickly.

**Battery Efficient:**

Designed for multi-hour races. Uses GPS efficiently and continues tracking while your screen is off. Typical battery usage is less than 10% per hour.

**Privacy:**

Your location is only tracked when you press Start. You choose what name to display. See our privacy policy at wstracker.org/privacy.html

**Requirements:**

- Event name and password from race organizers
- GPS-enabled device
- Mobile data or WiFi connection
- Location permission (required for tracking)

**Note:** This app is for organized events. You need the event password from race organizers to use it.

## Wear OS Specific Description

**Short Description (80 characters):**
GPS race tracking for your wrist - track windsurfing races from your watch.

**Full Description:**

Track your windsurfing races directly from your Wear OS smartwatch. Windsurfer Tracker for Wear OS provides standalone GPS tracking without needing your phone on the water.

**Features:**

- Standalone tracking over LTE or WiFi
- Large speed display in knots
- Request Assist emergency button
- Heart rate monitoring (if available)
- Works independently from phone
- Battery-efficient design

**Compatible Watches:**
- Google Pixel Watch (all models)
- Samsung Galaxy Watch 4 and newer
- Other Wear OS 3.0+ watches with GPS

Perfect for sailors who want to leave their phone on shore while still being tracked for safety.
